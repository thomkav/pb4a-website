## Who We Are

Portland Businesses For All is a coalition of business owners, entrepreneurs, and community members committed to building a more inclusive and equitable business environment in Portland.

## Our Values

- **Inclusivity**: We believe in creating opportunities for all businesses, regardless of size or background
- **Community**: We support policies that strengthen Portland's communities
- **Sustainability**: We promote business practices that are sustainable for our city's future
- **Equity**: We advocate for fair policies that benefit all Portlanders

## Get Involved

If you're interested in learning more or getting involved with Portland Businesses For All, please reach out to us.
